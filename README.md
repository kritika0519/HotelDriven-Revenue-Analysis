# HotelRevAI: AI-Driven Revenue Analysis for Hotels 🏨📊

An advanced Business Intelligence solution developed in **Power BI** to optimize hotel revenue, monitor occupancy trends, and predict booking risks. This project transforms complex hospitality data into actionable strategic insights.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview
**HotelRevAI** is designed to help hotel management groups understand their financial and operational performance through a multi-modular dashboard approach. By analyzing booking data, occupancy patterns, guest demographics, pricing effectiveness, and seasonal trends, the system provides a 360-degree view of hotel operations.

### ✨ Key Objectives
* **Revenue Optimization:** Monitoring key metrics including ADR, RevPAR, and total profit across branches and room categories.
* **Guest Intelligence:** Analyzing guest demographics and segmenting customers based on stay purpose and loyalty status.
* **Risk Mitigation:** Identifying booking trends, cancellation patterns, and using forecasting techniques to predict future occupancy.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Architecture
The project follows a robust BI lifecycle, from raw data ingestion to cloud deployment.



* **Data Modeling:** Implemented a Star Schema to consolidate hotel financial and operational performance data.
* **ETL Process:** Utilized **Power Query** for data transformation and cleaning from Excel sources.
* **DAX Calculations:** Developed custom measures for core KPIs such as `Total Profit`, `ADR`, `RevPAR`, and `Cancellation Rate`.
* **Predictive Analytics:** Integrated time-series forecasting to predict future occupancy and revenue trends with confidence intervals.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Modules

### 1. Performance Overview
A high-level executive summary of the hotel's financial health.
* **Metric Success:** Total Revenue reached **414.29M**, significantly exceeding the benchmark target.
* **Geographical Insight:** Bar charts reveal that Chennai and Bengaluru contribute the highest share of total revenue.

### 2. Revenue & Channel Analysis
Evaluating the effectiveness of distribution channels and stay patterns.
* **Channel Stability:** Revenue is well-distributed across OTA, Travel Agent, Corporate, and Direct channels.
* **Stay Duration:** A funnel chart analysis shows that longer stay durations positively impact overall profitability.

### 3. Guest Insights & Demographics
Understanding the composition and loyalty of the customer base.
* **Age Group Engagement:** Highest booking volumes are driven by the **46–60** and **26–35** age groups.
* **Global Reach:** Map visualizations confirm international customer reach across North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia.

### 4. Cancellation & Risk Forecasting
Predictive monitoring to protect the bottom line and support overbooking strategies.
* **Trend Analysis:** Daily cancellation rates typically fluctuate between **15%–22%**.
* **Segment Risk:** Decomposition trees identify higher cancellation volatility within the Business and Family segments.
